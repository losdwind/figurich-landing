常见困扰：不是你不够努力 Real Problems: Not a willpower issue

信息太多、时间太碎、方向太乱——靠堆课程解决不了 Too much info, no time, messy direction—buying more courses won’t fix it

状态：超时 TIMEOUT

刷手机停不下来 Phone scroll trap

想查一条信息，结果滑了半小时短视频。不是自控力差，而是环境在消耗你。 Open phone for one thing, lose 30 minutes. It’s not you; the environment is rigged to distract.

状态：存储已满 STORAGE_FULL

收藏一堆，却没动手 Saved links, zero action

课程、PDF、收藏夹都在涨，但作品产出没有涨。你需要的是清单和节奏，而不是更多资料。 More courses and PDFs, but no output. You need a checklist and rhythm, not more bookmarks.

状态：系统过载 OVERLOAD

AI 新东西太多 Too many AI updates

工具、提示词、最新版本满天飞，但真正能帮你省时间、赚到钱的那几个没有筛出来。 New tools and prompts every day, but the few that actually save time or make money are hard to spot.

状态：电量不足 LOW_BATTERY

一个人容易掉线 Hard to stay on track alone

没人提醒、没人共创，容易被琐事拉走。你需要外力帮你保持节奏，而不是鸡汤。 No reminders or partners, so chores win. You need gentle outside support, not more motivation quotes.

三步让注意力回正 Three steps to get your focus back

01

先清理 Clean the noise

一张清单关掉 90% 的通知和杂音，腾出连续的 30 分钟。第一天就能完成。 Use one checklist to shut down 90% of notifications and noise so you get 30 solid minutes. You can do this on day one.

状态：初始化中... STATUS: INITIALIZING...
02

再定方向 Pick a direction

用简单问答把"我擅长什么、想服务谁"写成一句话，明确接下来 4 周的输出主题。 Answer a few prompts to decide “what I’m good at” and “who I serve,” then lock a 4-week content theme.

状态：等待中... STATUS: PENDING...
03

最后用 AI 帮忙 Let AI assist

提供现成模板：回复邮件、整理资料、生成文案，AI 做基础体力，你负责决定和微调。 Plug in ready templates for replies, research tidy-up, and drafts. AI handles busywork; you make decisions and edits.

状态：锁定 STATUS: LOCKED
